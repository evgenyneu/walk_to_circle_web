Privacy Policy for Walk to Circle application

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Walk to Circle (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Evgenii Neumerzhictkii.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application does not obtain any user provided information.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application does not collect any information automatically with exception of the location of your device.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application collects precise information about the location of your mobile device in order to notify you when you reach a circle on the map. This location information never leaves your device and stays private.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Third parties do not have access to information obtained by the Application.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

Your data does not leave your device and stays private. Your data is removed from the device when the Application is unistalled. Therefore, we do not retain or manage your information.

Children

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13.

Security

All the data collected by the Application stays secure as it does not leave your device.

Contact us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us from the web site evgenii.com.
